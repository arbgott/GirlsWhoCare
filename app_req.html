Application & Requirements

Applications & Explanations


  • Welfare Application


Above is an application for the Welfare program.
Welfare is applicable to those who are unemployed, with an illness or disability, elderly, veterans and people with dependants such as children.
It can take a variety of forms such as monetary payments, subsidies, vouchers, and/or housing assistance.

  • Foodstamp Information

This is a food stamp application.
Food stamps are vouchers issued by the government to people with low income to exchange for food.
These stamps are accepted at most grocery stores across the country.
In select countries food stamps are accepted at fast food restaurants as well.
The link below the food stamp application will redirect you to a very helpful website dedicated to food stamps and the education on its surrounding topics and a list of establishments where they are accepted.

  • Medicare Applications

Medicare provides health insurance for Americans aged 65 and older, younger people with some disability status as determined by the Social Security Administration, as well as people with end stage renal disease and amyotrophic lateral sclerosis.


  • Medicaid Applications

Medicaid is a health care program that is intended to facilitate in paying for doctor visits, hospital stays, long-term medical, custodial care costs and other additional services for low-income families or individuals.
Medicaid is a joint program, funded mainly by the federal government and run at state level, where coverage may vary.
